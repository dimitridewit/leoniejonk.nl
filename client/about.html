About

Hello it’s nice to meet you. I’m Leonie, let me tell you a bit about myself.

The first time I ever sat down to work on a website, I was a teenager and the tool of choice was Geocities. I was mesmerized and built website after website. I never expected that this was something people could do as a job though. Since then I’ve come quite a long way. After 2 studies in the field of Interaction Design I’ve become fascinated with the way people interact with technology and the way it interacts with them.

Now I’m a Designer with 5+ years of experience in app- & webdesign. From interaction, through ux and into visual design. The full design package, as it were. I enjoy creating products that give joy to the people who use them and that make their world a little bit better, as you can see in the products that I’ve worked on so far.
