About

Flamyngo

The Flamyngo app is an app that makes it easy for users to ask friends for their favourite places and gather them all in one delightful place.

I worked closely with founder Maarten Graven to design the first version of the UI. Screens, icons and empty states were all designed. And if the reviews were any indication, users loved the clean and bright design.

Sadly the startup didn’t manage to grow the revenue they needed despite positive reviews and closed at the start of 2019.
